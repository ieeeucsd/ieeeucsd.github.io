IEEE at UC San Diego

Here at IEEE Student Branch at UC San Diego, we are dedicated to developing students into successful engineers by providing them with technical skills and personal development opportunities. We are a pre-professional organization and the 2nd largest student branch in the country, with over 350 members. This past year we held over 50 events, accommodating over 1500 students. These events include our four annual projects, three quarterly projects, hackathons, technical and professional development events, community social events and outreach programs. Through these opportunities, we have paved a way for students to network, collaborate and learn outside of the classroom. Being an IEEE student member has wonderful perks, including networking and career opportunities.
