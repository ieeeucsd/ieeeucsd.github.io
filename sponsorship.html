Become a Sponsor

IEEE Student Branch at UC San Diego is a non-profit organization focused on developing bright engineers to serve the community around us by hosting various on and off campus events, community outreach as well as entering into several technical project national competitions. We invite you to support help and be part of our efforts to strengthen our future scientists and engineers. We interacts extensively with our STEM community. The success and continuation of these programs requires the generosity of companies to support our efforts.

IEEE is a 501(c)(3) organization and can offer donors a tax deduction.
